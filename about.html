What We Do

The ACM student chapter of Penn State Behrend is a fairly new. It was founded in fall 2014 and soon after became active and popular. Our main focus is to promote education, teach students about various internships and job opportunities and prepare them for the world outside the classroom. We provide free tutoring for all students in various majors, we also offer speaker series to expose students to the latest computing research, trends and technologies. We host local hack days and other workshops. The ACM organization provides free travel to students to attend and participate in Hackathons and other group events such as Global Game Jam. If you are majoring in Computer Science, Electrical, Computer, or Software Engineering, we would like you to join us. Please follow us on Facebook and Twitter to learn more about our club.
