What We Do

The ACM chapter of Penn State Behrend is a fairly new club here on campus. The point of this club is to promote learning about the latest computing technology while also learning how to interact in a professional environment. This ACM chapter helps students learn by providing free tutoring, speak series, hosting local hack days, and various other work shops. To give students the ability to interact with others outside the class room, ACM provides free travel to various universities that host Hackathons, participate in group events such as Global Game Jam, and provide communication between various local companies. If you are majoring in Computer Science, Electrical, Computer, or software engineering. The ACM chapter would love to bring you aboard. If you would like to join our ranks Follow us on Facebook and Twitter and inquire about our weekly meetings. MICHAEL STUMPF
